Bio

I am a Lecturer in the College of Computer Science and Technology at Huaqiao University, China. I received my PhD degree in Information Security from Xidian University in 2019, advised by Prof. Yuqing Zhang. I was a visiting PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Arizona, advised by Prof. Ming Li. My research interests include Applied Cryptography and Privacy Enhancing Technologies.
